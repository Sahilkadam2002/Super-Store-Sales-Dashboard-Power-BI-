# Super-Store-Sales-Dashboard-Power-BI-
* The purpose behind to make this dashboard is to track the sales of Super Store to identify their monthly sales, sales by shiping and many other, with the help of intereactive powerr bi dashboard. 
## KPI's 
* Sales Performance Overview (Sales, Profit, Quantity)
* Identified sales by the category. 
* Sales by Segment Performance presented by pie chart.
* Time-Series Trends across months and years. 
* Dynamic Filters & Slicers for interactive exploration. 
* Presented month by month sales with the help of line chart. 
* Presented month by month profit with the help of line chart.
* Also added the map to identify sales by region.
